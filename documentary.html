The *Official* Banana Duck Nature Documentary

Title: The Official Banana Duck Nature Documentary

Episodes: 8

Total Duration: 2 hour and 47 minutes

Click Here to watch The Official Banana Duck Nature Documentary!

Episode Guide

Episode 1: Pilot

Duration: 22 minutes

What is a banana duck? This episode covers what a banana duck is, the identifying features of one, where they can be found, and basic terminology.

Episode 2: From Babana to Banana

Duration: 20 minutes

How do banana ducks change over their lifespan? This episode covers the differences between babana ducks and adult banana ducks, how they change over time, and their typical behaviors.

Episode 3: Mating Season

Duration: 18 minutes

How do banana ducks become couples? This episode covers when mating season is, typical mating rituals, what happens once two ducks are official a couple, and why they might break up.

Episode 4: Predator versus Prey

Duration: 19 minutes

What can hurt a banana duck, and what do banana ducks hurt? This episode covers what can be a predator to a banana duck, what can be prey to a banana duck, and the behaviors they show when pursuing or being pursued.

Episode 5: The Urban Jungle

Duration: 22 minutes

How do banana ducks act when they aren't in the wild? This episode covers the behaviors of banana ducks in the urban environment, when you might see them, and the best and worst ways to act around them.

Episode 6: The Natural Jungle

Duration: 21 minutes

How do banana ducks act when they are in the wild? This episode covers the behaviors of banana ducks when they are in a forest environment, when you might see them, and the best and worst ways to act around them.

Episode 7: Mutations and Misconceptions

Duration: 23 minutes

What do atypical banana ducks look like? This episode covers documented mutations of banana ducks, atypical behaviors, and misconceptions that people tend to have about banana duck features or behaviors.

Episode 8: Preserve and Conserve

Duration: 22 minutes

What struggles have banana ducks been facing in recent years? This episode covers the banana duck population drop, which locations have the largest decrease and fewest ducks, what is causing this, and what can be done to help them.
