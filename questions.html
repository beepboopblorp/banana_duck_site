Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)


Who is allowed admin acces?

Members of the *Official* Banana Duck Conservation Website are given the admin login and granted access.


Who are currently members of the *Official Banana Duck Conservation Website?

You can find the current members on slack, @eesha and @duck_tape. Contact them if you have any immediate questions, concerns, or suggestions about the website.

Current Admin Requests


This is the list of things that the *Offical* Banana Duck Conservation Website requests be created in the future in order to enhance the *Official* Banana Duck Conservation Website.

  • Quizzes to test readers on the knowledge they have gained about banana ducks and conservation.
  • Fun games that readers can play to immerse themselves in the banana duck experience.
  • A gallery of photographs of both banana duck photos pictured on the website and pictures taken out in the wild.
  • More research studies about biological facts and conservation efforts for banana ducks.
  • Database to store additional questions readers may have about banana ducks or the website.
